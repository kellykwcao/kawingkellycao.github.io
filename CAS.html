Project Description

For this internship with the ACS (American Chemistry Society) CAS Common Chemistry division, I worked alongside my mentor, Andrea Jacobs and internship partner, Amy Liu to create a lesson plan for undergraduate and high-level high school students to learn about the CAS Common Chemistry API and Cheminformatics. I was able to build on my Python programming skills, as well as learn about API's from industry and research professionals. At the end of the internship, Amy and I had the opportunity to present our project to a board of ACS members from different departments and at the 2022 ACS Spring Conference. It was incredibly rewarding to find out that our lesson plan was later implemented into many college curriculums and python toolkit utilized by CAS Common Chemistry team members for demo purposes.
